Data

In this demonstration, we are using Spatial Transcriptomics (ST) data published in Barkley et al.,2022. In that study, the dataset contains multiple samples from different cancer types, such as breast, gastrointestinal, liver, ovary, pancreas, endometrium, and others. This data is helpful to understand the heterogeneity in tumor micro environment (TME) among different cancer types. For this demonstration, we are using ten samples (3 breast, 2 gastrointestinal, 1 liver, 2 ovarian, 1 pancreas, and 1 endometrium) generated using SpaceRanger. We used Seurat (Hao et al.,2021) R package for data pre-processing and integrating the samples. With the integrated data, we did clustering followed by differential expression (DE) analysis to identify the marker genes of the clusters.
